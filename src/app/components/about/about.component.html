About Our Project

This blog is a capstone project for our bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Software Development and Security. The course is a senior-level program that allows us to work in teams to design, develop, test, implement, and document a semester-long project in a current computer science application area. Our blog is an example of a reusable and scalable application developed using the most current technologies and approaches.

Course Overview

Welcome to CMSC 495!
This course emphasizes teamwork, allowing us to simulate a working project environment. We have the opportunity to bring candidate projects for consideration or seek ideas from our instructor.

Course Learning Outcomes:

  • Conduct self-evaluations and peer reviews to improve collaborative projects.
  • Create user or technical documentation for an IT system or application.
  • Implement IT solutions that comply with specifications, requirements, standards, and guidelines.
  • Research, plan, conduct, and complete a collaborative, challenging, computer-related project in compliance with schedule deadlines.

We have worked together to design, develop, and deploy this blog application as part of our academic curriculum. We hope you enjoy exploring our work!

Cross-functional Collaboration

  • Regular Joint Meetings: Scheduled weekly to discuss progress, address challenges, and make collective decisions.
  • Decision-Making Framework: Decisions are made based on consensus among key stakeholders, with the Project Manager having the final say in case of disagreements.
  • Communication Channels: Utilizes a combination of regular meetings, email updates, and a project management tool (Discord) for ongoing communication and collaboration.
